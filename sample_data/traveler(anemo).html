Traveler (Anemo) Talents

Normal Attack

Foreign Ironwind

Normal Attack Performs up to 5 consecutive strikes. Charged Attack Consumes a certain amount of Stamina to unleash 2 rapid sword strikes. Plunging Attack Plunges from mid-air to strike the ground below, damaging enemies along the path and dealing AoE DMG upon impact.

Elemental Skill

Palm Vortex

Grasping the wind's might, you form a vortex of vacuum in your palm, causing continuous Anemo DMG to enemies in front of you. The vacuum vortex explodes when the skill duration ends, causing a greater amount of Anemo DMG over a larger area. Hold DMG and AoE will gradually increase Elemental Absorption If the vortex comes in contact with Hydro/Pyro/Cryo/Electro elements, it will deal additional elemental DMG of that type. Elemental Absorption may only occur once per use.

Elemental Burst

Gust Surge

Guiding the path of the wind currents, you summon a forward-moving tornado that pulls objects and opponents towards itself, dealing continuous Anemo DMG. Elemental Absorption If the tornado comes in contact with Hydro/Pyro/Cryo/Electro elements, it will deal additional elemental DMG of that type. Elemental Absorption may only occur once per use.

Traveler (Anemo) Passives

Ascension 1

Slitting Wind

The last hit of a Normal Attack combo unleashes a wind blade, dealing 60% of ATK as Anemo DMG to all opponents in its path.

Ascension 4

Second Wind

Palm Vortex kills renegerate 2% HP for 5s. This effect can only occur once every 5s.

Traveler (Anemo) Constellations

Constellation 1

Raging Vortex

Palm Vortex pulls in enemies within a 5m radius.

Constellation 2

Uprising Whirlwind

Increases Energy Recharge by 16%.

Constellation 3

Sweeping Gust

Increases the Level of Gust Surge by 3. Maximum upgrade level is 15.

Constellation 4

Cherishing Breezes

Reduces DMG taken while casting Palm Vortex by 10%.

Constellation 5

Vortex Stellaris

Increase the Level of Palm Vortex by 3. Maximum upgrade level is 15.

Constellation 6

Intertwined Winds

Targets who take DMG from Gust Surge have their Anemo RES decreased by 20%. If an Elemental Absorption occurred, then their RES towards the corresponding Element is also decreased by 20%.
