Lisa Talents

Normal Attack

Lightning Touch

Normal Attack Perform up to 4 lightning attacks that deal Electro DMG. Charged Attack Consumes a certain amount of Stamina to deal AoE Electro DMG after a short casting time. Plunging Attack Gathering the might of Electro, Lisa plunges towards the ground from mid-air, damaging all enemies in her path. Deals AoE Electro DMG upon impact with the ground.

Elemental Skill

Violet Arc

Channels the power of lightning to sweep bothersome matters away. Press Releases a homing Lightning Orb. On hit, it deals Electro DMG, and applies a stack of Conductive status (Max 3 stacks) to enemies in a small AoE. Hold After an extended casting time, calls down lightning from the heavens, dealing massive Electro DMG to all nearby enemies. Deals great amounts of extra damage to enemies based on the number of Conductive stacks applied to them, and clears their Conductive status.

Elemental Burst

Lightning Rose

Summons a Lightning Rose that unleashes powerful lightning bolts, launching surrounding enemies and dealing Electro DMG. The Lightning Rose will continuously emit lightning to knock back enemies and deal Electro DMG for so long as it persists.

Lisa Passives

Ascension 1

Induced Aftershock

Hits by charged Attacks apply Violet Arc's Conductive status to enemies.

Ascension 4

Static Electricity Field

Enemies hit by Lightning Rose have their DEF decreased by 15% for 10s.

Passive

General Pharmaceutics

When Lisa crafts a potion, she has a 20% chance to refund a portion of the crafting materials used.

Lisa Constellations

Constellation 1

Infinite Circuit

Lisa recovers 2 Energy for every opponent hit while holding Violet Arc. A maximum of 10 Energy can be regenerated in this manner at any one time.

Constellation 2

Electromagnetic Field

Holding Violet Arc has the following effects: Increases DEF by 25%. Increases Lisa's resistance to interruption.

Constellation 3

Resonant Thunder

Increases the Level of Lightning Rose by 3. Maximum upgrade level is 15.

Constellation 4

Plasma Eruption

Increases the number of lightning bolts released by Lightning Rose by 1-3.

Constellation 5

Electrocute

Increase the Level of Violet Arc by 3. Maximum upgrade level is 15.

Constellation 6

Pulsating Witch

When Lisa takes the field, she applies 3 stacks of Violet Arc's Conductive status onto nearby enemies. This effect can only occur once every 5s.
